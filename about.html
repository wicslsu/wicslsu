Women in Computer Science (WiCS) is an organization at Louisiana State University founded in 2015. WICS is committed to helping women to succeed in the Computer Science field.

Our activities include professional developments, academic workshops, social events, and more! We welcome everyone to join our events. Join us on TigerLink and Discord!

If you would like to contact WiCS, you can email us at wicslsu@gmail.com.
