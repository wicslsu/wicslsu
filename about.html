Women in Computer Science (WICS) is an organization at Louisiana State University founded in 2015. WICS is committed to helping women to succeed in the Computer Science field.

Our activities include resume workshops, tutoring sessions, career development, and learning sessions.

Women in Computer Science annually hosts a Game Jam in the fall of the year for students. The Game Jam is focused on allowing students to develop their skills and in a positive atmosphere.

If you would like to contact WICS, you can email us at wicslsu@gmail.com!
