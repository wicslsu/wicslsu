Becoming a Member

    To become a WiCS and/or WiCyS member, you need to...

  1. 1. Pay $20 for ENTIRE YEAR
  2. 2. Join TigerLink
  3. 3. Complete Hazing Prevention

After finishing the three major requirements, don't forget to fill out the sign up form!

Active Membership for WiCS

For WiCS, you are required to attend 8 events per semester OR 18 events per year.
For WiCyS, you are required to attend 3 events per semester OR 8 events per year.
