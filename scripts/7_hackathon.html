FAQ

What is Hackathon?

A hackathon is a time-limited coding event where participants collaborate to develop a project based on a theme. It encourages innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving as participants develop solutions.

When and where?

It takes place on March 21st, 2025 6:00 PM CST ~ March 23, 2025 6:00 PM CST at LSU PFT.

What is the theme?

IT'S A SECRET. Try guessing it.

What do I bring?

To survive, you only need your laptop and charger (and your keys!). However, if you want to be COMPETITIVE, bring a sleeping bag and a pillow. Warning: We have seen plenty walking zombies at PFT on Sunday morning.

I have no coding experience. Can I still come?

Of course! In fact, if you have absolutely no experience, this is the perfect time for you to learn how to code!

Is it free?

Yes! It's free for all. In addition, we provide food, and there will be prizes at the closing ceremony!
